Opioid Risk Tool Introduction

The Opioid Risk Tool (ORT) is a brief, self-report screening tool designed for use with adults patients in primary care settings to assess risk for opioid abuse among individuals prescibed opioids for treatment of chronic pain. Patients categorized as high-risk are at increased likelihood of future abusive drug-related behavior. The ORT can be adminsitered and scored in less than 1 minute and has been validated in both male and female patients, but not in non-pain populations.
